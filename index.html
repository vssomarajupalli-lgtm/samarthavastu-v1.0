📐 Magnetic Declination

Magnetic declination is the angle between Magnetic North (compass) and True North (astronomical).

If you used a compass to measure your plot direction, enter your city's declination value here. The software will auto-correct to True North.

If you used Google Maps or satellite — leave this at 0°. Satellite already gives True North.

Formula: True North = Compass Reading − Declination
